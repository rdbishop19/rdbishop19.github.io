Journey of the Sourcerer

I have never met The Sourcerer, nor The Sourceress.

I know not if they even have an earthly form. But their Path has been known since January 1, 1970 before the Dawn of the Silicon Age.

This Path, it dividesexponentially, sending each new Command Line Wanderer on his or her own unique odyssey.

What strange magic must bewitch this place!

Mr. Frost had but only two roads between which to choose. Such Boolean triviality! But for the lone Digital Traveler, the choices are infinite, wrought with divergence at every step.

Yet... in the end, all of these Paths meet at a singular terminal destination.

Some of you know of this place. Others have found clues hinting its existence, ever pulling you forward in your Path in search of its true name.

'Tis here, The Code Not Taken, where I find myself. Just as every traveler who has come before and every traveler yet to be.

So join me, Fellow Friend, and I promise you a journey forged in Bugs, Sweat, and Tears.

--The Glitchhiker
